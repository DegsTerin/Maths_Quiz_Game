## 2) Suggested tiers

Use these as monthly tiers:

1. `US$1/month` - `Supporter`  
   A simple way to support my work and help me keep building.
2. `US$3/month` - `Early Supporter`  
   Support ongoing development and get listed in my sponsor thanks section (optional).
3. `US$5/month` - `Backer`  
   Help fund maintenance, improvements, and new releases across my projects.
4. `US$10/month` - `Core Supporter`  
   Support my long-term open source and educational work in a bigger way.
5. `US$25/month` - `Project Sponsor`  
   For people who want to significantly support development and help me prioritize more time for public projects.
6. `US$50/month` - `Champion`  
   Major support for my open source and learning-focused projects.

## 3) Benefits you can safely offer

- Sponsor thank-you list in README or profile
- Early progress updates and roadmap posts
- Monthly sponsor-only development log
- Polls to vote on next feature/project focus
- Priority issue triage (no guaranteed SLA)

## 4) What to avoid in the beginning

- 1:1 mentorship included in low tiers
- Custom coding requests per sponsor
- Guaranteed response time promises
- Frequent live calls as mandatory rewards

## 5) Publishing checklist

1. Open `https://github.com/sponsors/DegsTerin/dashboard`.
2. Go to `Profile details` and paste the `Short bio` and `Introduction`.
3. Go to `Sponsor tiers` and create the suggested tiers.
4. Keep rewards lightweight for the first 30 days.
5. Add featured repositories/projects in your profile.
6. Publish and share your sponsor page link.
